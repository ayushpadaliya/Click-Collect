This repository contains an e-commerce application designed to facilitate seamless interactions between users and vendors. Users can browse through various categories and subcategories to explore a wide range of products available on the platform. They can view product details, add items to their cart, and place orders conveniently. Vendors, on the other hand, have the capability to manage their inventory by adding new categories, subcategories, and products. The application aims to provide a user-friendly experience for both buyers and sellers, fostering efficient transactions and enhancing the overall shopping experience.
